Welcome to My Personal Site

It’s now almost a decade that photography has been a way of seeing the world for me. As an experimental photographer I am just trying to click the shutter to delve into my surroundings regardless of paths and styles of photography. As an architect and urban designer, I’ve always been fascinated by ‘Natural Scene’ and what it contains including Hills, Landscape, Rivers, bridges, Monuments, and most importantly, the cause of its existence: Natural Beauty.

Born in Remote place called Dingla. It is located in Bhojpur (27°10′N 87°3′E / 27.167°N 87.05°E / 27.167; 87.05) is a town in Eastern Nepal. It is a place where Nepal's first Sanskrit School was established in 1932 B.S. by Guru Sadananda Adhikari. He was the Saint to learn,"Aasta Siddhi", Power, by his power the then ruler RANA's was fully impressed with him and asked him for his wish, it was the wish to establish Sanskrit School. He is also known as "The Light Of Knowledge" in Easter Region. I was Born in B.S. 2040 Magh 23. I got up and educated in Dingla for 2 years and then moved to India, Khursang (Down Boarding School) for 3 Years and then studied in Biratnagar, Nepal (Arniko Higher Secondary School) till my Schol Leaving Certoficate. My Further Studies continued in Kathmandu. I went to +2 in Classic International Academy them my Bachelor continued in Ratna Rajya Laxmi Campus till my Master Degree. I have Complede My Master in Major English and Bachelor in Journalism. Now I a employee in Nepal's top Leading Company, Nepal Life Insurance Company as an IT team, I work as Hardware and Networking section in the company from 03/01/2065 B.S and am still working for the company. My great hobby is to be a photographer, which i am still continuing as an part time.
